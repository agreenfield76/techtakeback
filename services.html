Important Notice - Accepted items

In order to have the greatest impact on our community, we have changed our services to focus on laptops and desktop computers only. These items allow us to have the greatest impact by promoting digital inclusion, and providing tech to those that need it most!

Unfortunately, many of the items previously accepted were not suitable for reuse or were not economically viable. Time spent disassembling and recycling items, or preparing 'low-value' items from reuse takes precious resources away from our reuse strategy. 

We have therefore amended our Accepted Items list to accept laptops & desktop computers only - please check your items can be accepted before attempting to donate.
